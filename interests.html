INTERESTS

Singing

I started singing when I was in elementary school, but I never took any singing lessons. However, everyone I know thinks my singing voice is very pleasant, which has given me a lot of confidence in my voice. This confidence has continued, and even now, in my first year of university, I still enjoy singing.

Listining to Music

Listening to music is an essential part of my life. Without music, my life would probably feel like a zombie existence. The music styles I often listen to include Japanese rock, electronic music, and J-pop. When I listen to music, I focus on how the producer arranges the song, so I don't often listen to popular music from my own country, Taiwan, because I feel like every song sounds similar and lacks variety.

Composing Music

I started producing music in junior high school, focusing primarily on electronic styles like Future Bass and House. Recently, I've been really interested in creating Japanese-style music, though it's quite challenging, so I still need more time to learn. Since I began, I've been entirely self-taught in music production, covering software operation, mixing, arranging, and more. Recently, I've also considered studying more advanced music theory, including harmony, as I feel my current foundation might not be enough for deeper music production.

Exploring Softwares

I've always loved exploring various software, as they’re like convenient, handy tools for me. By now, I’ve worked with a wide range of software, such as Photoshop for photo editing, DaVinci Resolve for video editing, Blender 3D for 3D modeling, Unity 3D for game development, and Fruity Loops Studio as a digital audio workstation. I’ve also discovered many helpful websites to meet my everyday needs. Recently, I’ve come across various AI tools that help me complete most tasks even faster.

Gaming

Although I don’t play games much, I do play some during my free time or when I want to procrastinate. Recently, I’ve been playing first-person shooters like CS2, and in the past, I used to play some RPGs or competitive games. Since starting university, I've had a lot more free time, so I've been able to spend more time playing and exploring games that I enjoy.
